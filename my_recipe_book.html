Low-Carb Vanilla Ice Cream

Yield: 6 scoops (~½ cup each)

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and allulose until smooth and slightly pale.
  2. Heat almond milk until steaming (don’t boil).
  3. Temper the eggs by slowly whisking the hot milk into the yolk mixture.
  4. Cook the base: Return everything to the pan and cook on low, stirring constantly, until it lightly thickens (170–175°F / 77–80°C).
  5. Flavor + stabilize: Remove from heat and whisk in vanilla, salt, and xanthan gum.
  6. Chill the custard completely in the fridge (2–4 hours).
  7. Freeze step 1: Pour into a shallow dish and freeze for 30–45 minutes.
  8. Break up ice: Whisk or blend, then return to freezer.
  9. Repeat mixing every 30–45 minutes for 3–4 rounds.
  10. Final freeze until firm and scoopable.

Quick Notes

  • **Allulose** keeps the texture soft and scoopable.
  • **Xanthan gum** reduces iciness and adds creaminess.
